Markers

Markers are a sort of simplified qr-codes. On this web-app specific 3D scenes have been defined for specific markers, so when the camera recognizes a marker, the web-app shows the 3D model on top of it. You can access the markers for different categories below

Alphabet

Markers containing the English Alphabet will be shown on the screen, use the AR Scanner through your phone to scan and we the 3D model for corresponding alphabet on top of the marker

Shapes

Markers containing Shapes will be shown on the screen, use the AR Scanner through your phone to scan and we the 3D model for corresponding shape on top of the marker

Body

Markers containing the parts of the body will be shown on the screen, use the AR Scanner through your phone to scan and we the 3D model for corresponding body part on top of the marker
